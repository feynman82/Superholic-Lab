Common questions

Pricing FAQ.

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?
No. Your 7-day free trial starts the moment you create an account — no payment details required. You only enter your card when you decide to subscribe.
What happens when my free trial ends?
When your 7-day trial ends, you'll need to subscribe to continue practising. We will never charge you automatically — you choose when to subscribe by clicking Subscribe above.
Can I change or cancel my plan?
Yes. Once you subscribe, you can upgrade, downgrade, update your payment method, or cancel at any time from your account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. All paid plans come with a 7-day no-questions-asked refund. Email support@superholiclab.com within 7 days of your charge and we'll process the refund — no forms, no friction.
Is my child's data secure?
Yes. We're fully PDPA-compliant. All student data is encrypted at rest and in transit, stored on Singapore servers (Supabase SG region). We do not sell your data and never will.
