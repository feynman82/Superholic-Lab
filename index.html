I don't understand why the answer to Q4 is B and not A. Both seem right to me?
Great question — let's think it through together. 🤔 In option A, the force is applied at the pivot point. What does that tell us about the moment arm?
Oh… the moment arm would be zero? So there's no turning effect?
Exactly right! 🌟 In PSLE, the keyword the examiner is looking for here is "moment arm". A zero moment arm = zero turning effect. That's why A cannot cause movement — and why B is the correct answer. Does that make sense?
