Our Story

We built the tutor we wished we had.

Superholic Lab wasn't created by a faceless corporation. It was built by Singaporean educators and engineers who wanted to solve a single, frustrating problem: students memorising answers instead of understanding concepts.

The Problem with "Practice Makes Perfect"

In Singapore's highly competitive education landscape, parents are told that doing endless past-year papers is the key to success. But doing 1,000 questions means nothing if a child repeats the same conceptual mistake 1,000 times.

Most platforms and assessment books simply provide an answer key: "Q1: B". If a student chooses 'A', they are left guessing why their logic was flawed. Without intervention, that misconception hardens, leading to anxiety and poorer exam results.

The Superholic Solution

We engineered Superholic Lab to focus entirely on the "Why". We don't just tell students they are wrong; our AI engine breaks down the exact trap they fell into.

Every single MCQ option in our database has a dedicated explanation. Every open-ended question comes with a step-by-step worked solution using strict MOE marking-scheme terminology. Mistakes are no longer dead ends; they are precise learning opportunities.
