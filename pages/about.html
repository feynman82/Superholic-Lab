Our Story

Why we built Superholic Lab

The bill arrived every month like clockwork. Two kids, three subjects each, six tuition slots a week — and still, the quiet anxiety of wondering whether it was enough.

Superholic Lab began with a simple frustration: why does exam-ready practice cost a fortune, and why does it only happen twice a week in someone else's living room? Singapore children sit high-stakes exams. They need to practise constantly — on the bus, between activities, after dinner. Not just when the tutor is available.

So we built what we wished existed. A platform that generates unlimited, MOE-aligned practice for any topic, any level, any time — for a fraction of what a single tuition session costs. No scheduling. No travelling. No waiting.

Every child deserves to walk into the exam hall having practised more than the kid next to them. That is what Superholic Lab is for.
