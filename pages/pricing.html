Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when my free trial ends? +
You will automatically be billed for the plan you selected. If you cancel before the 7 days are up, you will not be charged a single cent.
Can I change plans later? +
Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time directly from your billing dashboard.
Is my child's data secure? +
Absolutely. We are fully compliant with the Singapore PDPA. All student performance data is encrypted and securely stored in local Singapore servers (Supabase SG region).
How many subjects are included in the Single plan? +
The Single plan allows you to choose exactly one subject (Mathematics, Science, or English) for your child. You can switch the chosen subject at the start of your billing cycle.
