Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 March 2026

1. Who we are

Superholic Lab is an online educational platform operated by Superholic Lab Pte. Ltd., a company incorporated in Singapore ("we", "us", "our"). Our platform provides MOE-aligned practice questions, worked solutions, and an AI tutoring service for students in Singapore.

For any privacy enquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@superholiclab.com.

2. Scope of this policy

This policy applies to all personal data collected through www.superholiclab.com and our associated mobile-optimised web application. It governs how we handle the personal data of parents, guardians, and the children who use the platform (collectively, "users").

We comply with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) and act as the Data Controller for all personal data processed by us.

3. Data we collect

Account data — when you sign up, we collect your full name and email address. Children's profiles are created by parents or guardians and require only a first name and school level.

Usage data — quiz results, topics practised, scores, and AI tutor conversation history. This data is used exclusively to personalise your child's learning experience and to show you progress reports.

Payment data — billing and payment details are processed directly by Stripe, Inc. We never store credit card numbers or full payment credentials on our servers. We receive only a Stripe customer ID and subscription status.

Technical data — your IP address, browser type, and anonymised page-view events collected by Plausible Analytics (a privacy-first analytics provider that does not use cookies or cross-site tracking).

4. How we use your data

  • To create and manage your account and subscription.
  • To deliver personalised practice questions and track your child's progress.
  • To provide the AI tutor service (conversation messages are sent to Anthropic's API for processing; see Section 6).
  • To process payments and prevent fraud.
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, subscription receipts).
  • To improve the platform through aggregated, anonymised analytics.

We do not use your personal data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with any party other than those described in Section 6.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on servers located in Singapore (AWS ap-southeast-1 region). We use row-level security policies to ensure that users can only access their own data.

All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt via Supabase Auth and are never stored in plaintext. We conduct regular security reviews and follow industry best practices.

6. Sharing with third parties

We share personal data only with the following service providers, each of whom is contractually required to protect your data:

  • Supabase, Inc. — database, authentication, and storage.
  • Stripe, Inc. — payment processing. Subject to Stripe's own Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic, PBC — AI responses for the AI tutor feature. Tutor conversation messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train models. Please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel, Inc. — web hosting and serverless functions.
  • Plausible Analytics — privacy-first, cookie-free website analytics. No personal data is stored.

7. Children's data

Our platform is designed for use by children under the supervision and consent of a parent or guardian. Accounts are created by parents or guardians, who are responsible for ensuring the platform is used appropriately.

We collect only the minimum data necessary for children's profiles (first name and school level). We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data from children without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us at privacy@superholiclab.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your rights under the PDPA

Under Singapore's PDPA, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of any inaccurate personal data.
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw your consent to the collection, use, or disclosure of your personal data. Note that withdrawal may affect our ability to provide the service.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@superholiclab.com.

9. Data retention

We retain your account and usage data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription and request deletion, we will delete all personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., financial records for 5 years under the Companies Act).

10. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Our analytics provider (Plausible) is cookie-free. Supabase Auth uses localStorage to persist your session on your device; this is a functional necessity and not used for tracking.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or by displaying a prominent notice on the platform. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent revision.
